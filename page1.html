Solutions

One way schools could improve safety is by asking for help from the government and people in the local community. Fundraisers could also help pay for safer tools.

Government + education funding

Request educational funds and make sure they are used to purchase updated safety tools and equipment.

Local partnerships + expert help

Work with businesses and groups to donate safe tools or send experts to teach proper safety practices.

School fundraisers

Run school fundraisers to buy or replace unsafe tools, and improve the learning environment.
